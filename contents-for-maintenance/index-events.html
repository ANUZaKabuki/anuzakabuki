INTRODUCTION

This play follows the story of the dashing protagonist, Danshichi Kurobē, a commoner who defends ordinary townspeople against the warrior class. Having been recently released from jail after wounding a retainer under the notorious Ōtori Sagaemon, Danshichi reunites with his family and friends while his villaninous father-in-law plans to steal and sell off the lover of Isonojō, the samurai who saved Danshichi’s life. Get ready for a thrilling action and comedy pla, coming mid December 2021!
