INTRODUCTION

When an imperial messenger arrives at the household of Lord Ono-no Harumichi, the clan is thrown into chaos. The precious Tanzaku which is said to bring down rainfall has gone missing! Meanwhile, a messenger from one Lord Toyohide, Kumedera Danjo, has arrived at the household, to confront Harumichi about his daughter Nishikinomae, and the illness which prevents her from being betrothed to Toyohide. Will they be able to recover the Tanzaku and cure the princess of her illness?
